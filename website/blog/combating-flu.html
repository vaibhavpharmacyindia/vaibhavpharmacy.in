Combating the Flu Season in Lucknow: Preventive Tips and Essential Supplements

By Vaibhav Pharmacy Team May 15, 2026

As the seasons shift in Uttar Pradesh, the sudden changes in temperature often bring an uptick in viral fevers, common colds, and the flu. Being proactive is the best way to protect yourself and your family.

1. Boost Your Immunity Through Nutrition

A strong immune system is your first line of defense. Focus on a diet rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants. Incorporate local citrus fruits, leafy greens, and nuts into your daily meals.

2. Essential Supplements for Flu Season

Sometimes, diet isn't enough to get all the required nutrients. Consider the following supplements (after consulting with your doctor):

  • Vitamin C with Zinc: These are powerful antioxidants that help fortify immune defenses.
  • Multivitamins: To fill in any nutritional gaps in your daily diet.
  • Ayurvedic Immune Boosters: Trusted natural formulations like Chyawanprash or Giloy extracts can provide natural resistance.

3. Basic Hygiene Practices

Simple habits can drastically reduce your chances of catching a viral infection. Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. Keep a small bottle of alcohol-based hand sanitizer in your pocket when traveling around Lucknow. And always remember to cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when coughing or sneezing.
