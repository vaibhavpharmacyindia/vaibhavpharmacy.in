Lucknow mein Flu Season se Kaise Bachein: Preventive Tips aur Zaroori Supplements

By Vaibhav Pharmacy Team Feb 10, 2026

Uttar Pradesh mein jaise-jaise mausam badalta hai, viral fever, sardi-zukham aur flu ke cases achanak badh jaate hain. Is mausam mein pehle se tyar rehna hi khud ko aur family ko safe rakhne ka sabse achha tarika hai.

1. Sahi Nutrition se Badhayein Apni Immunity

Ek strong immune system aapka pehla defense hai. Apni diet mein Vitamin C aur antioxidants shaamil karein. Rozana ke khane mein local citrus fruits (khatte fal), hari sabziyan aur dry fruits zarur khayein.

2. Flu Season ke liye Zaroori Supplements

Kabhi-kabhi sirf diet se pure nutrients nahi mil paate. Aise mein in supplements ke baare mein socha ja sakta hai (apne doctor se poochhne ke baad):

  • Vitamin C aur Zinc: Yeh powerful antioxidants hote hain jo body ka immune system majboot banate hain.
  • Multivitamins: Agar aapki normal diet mein kuch kami hai toh multivitamins use puri karte hain.
  • Ayurvedic Immune Boosters: Chyawanprash ya Giloy extract jaisi natural Ayurvedic dawaaiyan natural resistance badhane mein madad karti hain.

3. Basic Hygiene Habits

Kuch simple aadatein aapko viral infection se bacha sakti hain. Apne haath kum-se-kum 20 seconds tak sabun aur paani se zarur dhoyein. Jab bhi Lucknow mein bahar niklein, pocket mein ek chhota alcohol-based hand sanitizer rakhein. Aur hamesha khaste ya chhinkte waqt apna muh tissue ya kohni se dhakein.
