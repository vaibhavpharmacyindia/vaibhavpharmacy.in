10 Zaroori Medicines Jo Lucknow ke Har Ghar mein Honi Chahiye

By Vaibhav Pharmacy Team May 26, 2026

Lucknow ke har ghar mein ek "dawai ka dabba" toh zaroor hoga: ek chhota sa box jisme alag-alag kism ki dawaaiyon ke strips pade rehte hain... adhi expired ho chuki hain, aur baaki pata hi nahi kis cheez ki hain.

Kuch jaana-pehchana laga?

Ghar par ek organized medicine kit rakhna srif convenience ki baat nahi hai, ye kisi raat ko medical store par daudne ya pareshan hone se bacha sakta hai. Yeh hai aisi 10 zaroori dawaion aur items ki ek practical guide, jo Lucknow walo ko apne ghar mein zaroori rakhna chahiye.

1. Paracetamol (Fever aur Pain ke liye)

Ye kisi bhi ghar ki sabse basic aur sabse zyada zaroori dawai hai. Paracetamol fever, headache, body ache aur halke pain mein madad karta hai. Bado ke liye 500mg tablets aur agar ghar mein bachhe hain, to paediatric syrup dono zarur rakhein.

  • Common brands: Crocin, Dolo, Calpol (bachhon ke liye)

Zaroori Baat: Dose kabhi extra na lein. Agar fever 3 din se jada rehta hai, doctor se consult karein.

2. ORS Packets (Oral Rehydration Salts)

Lucknow ki garmi sab jante hain. Garmi, diarrhea, ya vomiting ki wajah se dehydration bahut aam baat hai, khas karke bacchon aur badho ke aaspaas. ORS ek simple, jaan bachane wala nuskha hai jo har ghar mein hona zaroori hai.

3. Antacid (Acidity aur Gas ke liye)

Lucknow ke shaukeen aur nawaabo waale khane (kisko tunday kabab yad aya?) ka natija acidity hi banta hai. Gelusil, Digene, ya Eno jaise antacid turant aaram dete hain.

4. Antihistamine (Allergies ke liye)

Mausami allergies, dust allergies, keede ka katna ya skin rashes: Cetirizine ya Levocetirizine jaise antihistamine bohot kaam aate hain. Lucknow ke badalte mausam (February-March aur October-November) mein iski khaas zarurat hoti hai.

5. Antiseptic Solution aur Bandages

Halki-phulki chot ya jalna am baat hai, khaas karke agar ghar mein bachhe hon. Antiseptic liquid (Dettol ya Savlon), cream (Betadine), cotton aur adhesive bandages humesha aas pass rakhne chahiye.

6. Thermometer

Waise toh yeh dawai nahi hai, par zarurat mein iski sabse zyada ahamiyat hai. Ek digital thermometer bukhar naapne mein accurately madad karta hai.

7. Anti-diarrheal (Loose Motions ke liye)

Stomach infections Lucknow mein common hain, khaas monsoons (July-Sept) mein jab paani se hone wali beemari badh jaati hai. Loperamide jaisi dawai aapki bimaari manage kar sakti hai.

8. Cough Syrup aur Cold Medicine

Sookhi khasi aur wet/productive khasi dono ki syrup kit mein zaroor honi chahiye. Sardiyon mein lozenges aur nasal decongestant drops bhi zaroori hotey hain.

9. Pain Relief Spray ya Gel (Muscle Pain ke liye)

Diclofenac gel (Volini, Voveran) ya koi bhi pain relief spray muscle pain, sprains aur backache ke kaam ata hai.

10. Prescription Medicines (Chronic Conditions)

Agar aapke ghar mein kisi ko diabetes, hypertension ya koi chronic beemari ki dawa di jati hai, unke paas kabhi bhi ek hafte se kam stock mat chorna!
