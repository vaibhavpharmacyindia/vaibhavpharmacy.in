Holi Ke Baad Skin Ka Haal Kharab? Yeh 7 Tips Apnao, Glow Wapas Lao

By Vaibhav Pharmacy | Sehat Diary March 5, 2026 6 min read

Holi khatam. Masti khatam. Ab jo bacha hai woh hai: chehra pe jamaa hua pakka rang, haath jo sabun se bhi saaf nahi ho rahe, aur skin jo itni dry ho gayi hai ki lagta hai Lucknow ki garmi already aa gayi.

Sounds familiar?

Har saal same story hoti hai. Holi mein full enjoy karte hain, lekin uske baad skin ko aise treat karte hain jaise woh steel ka bartan ho: ghis-ghis ke ragadna, harsh sabun lagana, aur fir sochna ki "rang kyun nahi ja raha?"

Aaj ka blog specifically isi ke liye hai. Holi 2026 ke baad apni skin aur baalon ko kaise recover karein - simple, ghar pe available cheezein use karke.

Pehle Samjho: Rang Itna Khatarnak Kyun Hota Hai?

Zyaadatar market mein milne wale Holi colours organic nahi hote. Inme synthetic dyes hoti hain, kuch mein lead oxide, mercury, aur industrial chemicals bhi milaye jaate hain. Jab yeh chemicals skin pe ghanton baithe rehte hain, toh yeh hota hai:

  • Skin ka natural oil layer strip ho jaata hai
  • Pores clog ho jaate hain
  • Micro-tears ban jaate hain zyada ragadne se

Result? Dryness, rashes, itching, kabhi kabhi full-blown allergic reaction. Isliye post-Holi care sirf "rang chudana" nahi hai. Yeh skin barrier ko repair karna hai.

Tip 1: Pehle Oil Lagao, Baad Mein Dhona

Yeh sabse important step hai jo log skip kar dete hain.

Rang chudane ke liye seedha sabun aur paani mat use karo. Pehle nariyal tel (coconut oil) ya badam tel (almond oil) leke skin pe halke haath se massage karo. 10-15 minute chhod do.

Oil kya karta hai: yeh rang ke particles ko loosen karta hai bina skin ko damage kiye. Jab aap baad mein dhote ho, toh rang aasaani se nikalta hai aur skin ka moisture bhi bana rehta hai.

💡 Pro tip: Sarson ka tel bhi kaam karta hai, especially agar rang bahut pakka ho. Lucknow ke ghar mein toh sarson ka tel hota hi hai.

Tip 2: Besan + Dahi Ka Magic Pack

Yeh aapki nani ka nuskha hai, aur science bhi isko back karti hai.

2 tablespoon besan mein 1 tablespoon dahi mila ke paste bana lo. Isko face aur haathon pe lagao. 15 minute chhodo, fir halke haathon se circular motion mein ragad ke dholo.

Besan gentle exfoliant ka kaam karta hai - dead skin aur rang dono nikalte hain. Dahi mein lactic acid hota hai jo skin ko soothe karta hai aur moisture restore karta hai.

⚠️ Important: Isko zyada force se mat ragdo. Holi ke baad skin already sensitive hoti hai. Gentle rehna hai.

Tip 3: Gunguna Paani Use Karo, Garam Nahi

Bahut log sochte hain ki garam paani se rang jaldi niklega. Galat.

Garam paani skin ko aur zyada dry karta hai aur rang ko "set" kar deta hai - matlab rang skin mein aur deep jaata hai. Gunguna (lukewarm) paani use karo nahane ke liye. Isse pores open hote hain bina skin ko damage kiye.

Aur ek baat: nahane ke turant baad moisturizer lagana mat bhoolna. Wet skin pe moisturizer lagane se absorption best hoti hai.

Tip 4: Aloe Vera Gel Lagao Raat Ko

Agar Holi ke baad skin pe jalan, redness, ya halki swelling hai, toh aloe vera gel sabse acha soother hai.

Fresh aloe vera patta le sakte ho (bahut se Lucknow ke gharon mein milta hai) ya pharmacy se pure aloe vera gel khareed sakte ho. Raat ko sone se pehle affected areas pe lagao. Yeh skin ko cool karega, inflammation reduce karega, aur overnight healing mein help karega.

Tip 5: Baalon Ko Bhoolna Mat

Baal Holi mein sabse zyada suffer karte hain. Rang, paani, dhoop - sab milke baalon ko straw jaisa bana dete hain.

  • Step 1: Baalon mein nariyal ya badam tel laga ke 30-45 minute chhodo.
  • Step 2: Mild shampoo se dho lo. 2-3 baar shampoo karne ki zaroorat nahi hai, ek baar kaafi hai.
  • Step 3: Conditioner zaroor lagao. Tips pe focus karo. 5 minute chhod ke dholo.
  • Step 4: Agla 2-3 din baalon ko heat styling (straightener, dryer) se door rakho.

Agar rang bahut pakka hai aur ek baar mein nahi nikla, toh relax. 2-3 wash mein nikal jaayega. Force se nikalne ki koshish mein baal toot sakte hain.

Tip 6: Andar Se Bhi Hydrate Karo

Skin care sirf bahar se nahi hota. Holi ke baad 2-3 din extra hydration zaruri hai.

  • Din mein 3-4 litre paani piyo
  • Nariyal paani excellent option hai - electrolytes bhi milte hain
  • Tarbuz aur santara bhi khao - natural antioxidants hote hain
  • Heavy fried food avoid karo - 2-3 din halka khana khao: dal, chawal, dahi, sabzi

Tip 7: Jab Ghar Ke Nuskhe Se Kaam Na Bane

Kabhi kabhi Holi ke baad simple dryness ya halka rang nahi hota. Kuch logon ko serious allergic reactions ho sakti hain. Dhyan rakhna hai agar yeh symptoms dikhein:

  • Skin pe red patches ya chhale (blisters) ban rahe hain
  • Itching itni hai ki neend nahi aa rahi
  • Aankh mein jalan ya redness hai
  • Skin se fluid aa raha hai
  • Chehra ya haath pe swelling dikh rahi hai

Agar yeh mein se kuch bhi ho, toh ghar ka nuskha chhodo aur doctor se milo. Aur beech mein agar koi antihistamine (allergy ki dawa) ya calamine lotion chahiye, toh Vaibhav Pharmacy se turant mangwa lo.

⛔ Kabhi bhi nimbu (lemon) ya vinegar jaise acidic cheezein irritated skin pe mat lagao. Internet pe bahut log suggest karte hain, lekin yeh sensitive skin pe chemical burn kar sakta hai.

📋 Quick Checklist: Holi Ke Baad Kya Karna Hai

✅ Haan Karo:

  • Pehle oil se rang loosen karo, baad mein dhona
  • Besan + dahi ka pack use karo
  • Gungune paani se nahao
  • Aloe vera ya calamine lotion lagao
  • Baalon mein tel lagao, mild shampoo use karo
  • 3-4 litre paani daily piyo
  • 2-3 din halka khana khao

❌ Nahi Karna Hai:

  • Harsh sabun ya detergent se skin ragadna
  • Garam paani se nahana
  • Nimbu ya vinegar irritated skin pe lagana
  • Ek hi din mein 4-5 baar shampoo karna
  • Allergic reaction ko ignore karna

  • Pure aloe vera gel (cooling aur healing ke liye)
  • Mild moisturizers aur lip balm
  • Antihistamine tablets (doctor ki salah se lein)
  • Medicated shampoo (scalp irritation ke liye)
