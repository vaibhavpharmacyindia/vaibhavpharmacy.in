The Essential At-Home First Aid Kit Checklist for Lucknow Households

By Vaibhav Pharmacy Team May 24, 2026

Accidents happen when we least expect them. Whether it's a minor burn while cooking in the kitchen or a scraped knee from playing outside in the colony, having a well-stocked first aid kit can make all the difference in handling the situation calmly and effectively.

1. Wound Care Essentials

  • Adhesive Bandages (Band-Aids): Various sizes for minor cuts and scrapes.
  • Sterile Gauze Pads and Rolls: Essential for covering larger wounds and stopping bleeding.
  • Medical Tape: To secure gauze and bandages in place.
  • Antiseptic Liquid: Such as Savlon or Dettol to clean wounds and prevent infection.

2. Tools & Accessories

  • Digital Thermometer: Necessary for quickly checking for fever.
  • Tweezers & Small Scissors: For removing splinters and cutting medical tape or gauze.
  • Disposable Gloves: Protects both the caregiver and the patient from cross-contamination.

3. Essential Over-The-Counter (OTC) Medications

  • Pain Relievers & Fever Reducers: Paracetamol or Ibuprofen.
  • Antacids/Digestion Drops: For sudden stomach upsets or acidity.
  • Burn Cream: Such as Silverex or Burnol for immediate relief from minor kitchen burns.
  • Muscle Pain Spray/Ointment: Volini or Moov for sudden sprains.

Disclaimer: Always consult a healthcare professional for persistent symptoms. The above list is for informational purposes only.
