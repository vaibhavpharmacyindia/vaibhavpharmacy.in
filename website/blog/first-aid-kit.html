Lucknow Households ke liye Zaroori At-Home First Aid Kit Checklist

By Vaibhav Pharmacy Team May 24, 2026

Accidents bata kar nahi aate. Chahe kitchen mein khana banate waqt halka sa jal jana ho yya colony mein khelte waqt bachhon ko chot lag jana ho, ek achhi tarah se equipped first aid kit hone se aap situation ko shanti aur sahi tarike se handle kar sakte hain.

1. Wound Care (Ghaav ke liye) Essentials

  • Adhesive Bandages (Band-Aids): Chhoti chot aur scrapes ke liye har size ki band-aids.
  • Sterile Gauze Pads aur Rolls: Bade ghaav ko cover karne aur bleeding rokne ke liye zaroori hai.
  • Medical Tape: Gauze aur bandages ko apni jagah par set karne ke liye.
  • Antiseptic Liquid: Jaise Savlon ya Dettol, taki ghaav ko saaf karke infection se bachaya ja sake.

2. Tools & Accessories

  • Digital Thermometer: Fever check karne ke liye sabse zaroori item.
  • Tweezers & Small Scissors: Kaante (splinters) nikalne aur medical tape ya gauze ko kaatne ke liye.
  • Disposable Gloves: Yeh caregiver aur marij dono ko cross-contamination se bachata hai.

3. Zaroori Over-The-Counter (OTC) Medicines

  • Pain Relievers & Fever Reducers: Paracetamol ya Ibuprofen body pain aur bukhar ke liye.
  • Antacids/Digestion Drops: Achanak pet kharab hone ya acidity ki problem ke liye.
  • Burn Cream: Jaise Silverex ya Burnol, kitchen burns par turant aaram ke liye.
  • Muscle Pain Spray/Ointment: Achanak moch aa jane par Volini ya Moov jaisi creams.

Disclaimer: Agar problems theek naa ho, toh hamesha ek healthcare professional ya doctor se consult karein. Yeh list sirf general information ke liye hai.
