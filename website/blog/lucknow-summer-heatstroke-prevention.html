Lucknow ki Garmi aur Loo Se Bachaav: Kya Karein, Kya Na Karein

March 17, 2026 5 min read By Expert Pharmacist

Lucknow ki garmi aur usme chalne wali 'Loo' se bachna aasan nahi hai. Jab temperature 40°C cross kaarta hai, toh body ka dehydration level bahut jaldi badh jata hai. UP ki is harsh garmi mein heatstroke (Loo lagna) ek common issue ban jata hai, khaas karke baccho aur buzurgo ke liye.

Aaj Vaibhav Pharmacy ke health experts aapko batayenge ki is summer season mein apni family ko loo se kaise surakshit rakhein aur kya zaroori cheezein aapke ghar par humesha honi chahiye.

Heatstroke (Loo) Ke Lakshan (Symptoms)

Sabse pehle yeh janna zaroori hai ki loo lagne ke kya lakshan hote hain taaki aap turant action le sakein:

  • Bahut zyada paseena aana ya achaanak paseena aana band ho jana.
  • Tez sir dard aur chakkar aana.
  • Ulti (vomiting) ya jee machalna (nausea).
  • Heartbeat tez hona aur saans phulna.
  • Bolne mein takleef hona ya behoshi mehsus hona.

Alert

Agar kisi ko ye lakshan dikhein, toh unhe turant kisi thandi jagah par le jayein, unke kapde thode dheele karein aur geely kapde se unki body ponchein. Zaroorat padne par turant doctor se sampark karein.

Loo Se Bachne Ke Super Tips: Kya Karein?

1. Hydration Hai Sabse Zaroori

Garmiyon mein paani hi aapka sabse bada dost hai. Din bhar mein kam se kam 3-4 litre paani piyein. Pyaas na lagne par bhi thoda-thoda paani peete rahein.

Summer Pharmacy Essentials

Normal paani ke alawa, apni body mein electrolytes ka balance banaye rakhne ke liye inka use karein:

  • ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts): 1 litre paani mein milakar poore din use karein. (Aapko ORS delivery in vrindavan yojna hamare yahan se mil jayegi).
  • Electoral Powder: Energy aur hydration ke liye perfect.
  • Glucon-D & Glucose: Instant energy ke liye bahut faydemand hai.

2. Khane Peene Mein Badlaav

Summer mein light khana khayein. Taseer thandi hone wali cheezein jaise tarbooz (watermelon), khira (cucumber), lassi, chhaach, aur nariyal paani ka sevan badha dein. Bahar ka oily aur masaledaar khana avoid karein.

3. Pura Cover Karke Niklein

Agar dopehar mein 12 baje se 4 baje ke beech nikalna bahut zaroori ho, toh head cover karke niklein (cap, umbrella ya cotton dupatta). Full sleeves ke light color wale cotton kapde pehnein. Aur bahar nikalne se 20 minute pehle sunscreen zaroor lagayein.

Kya Na Karein (What to Avoid)

  • Tez dhoop se direct AC wale room mein na jayein, aur na hi wahan se sidha bahar dhoop mein niklein.
  • Bahut zyada chai, coffee ya alcohol avoid karein, kyunki ye body ko dehydrate karte hain.
  • Dopahar ki dhoop mein heavy exercise karne se bachein.
  • Khali pet kabhi bahar garmi mein na niklein.

Aakhiri Salah

Prevention is always better than cure. Thodi si savdhani aur sahi diet plans apnane se, aap is summer ko bina kisi takleef ke enjoy kar sakte hain. Always keep stock of ORS and glucose powders at home.
