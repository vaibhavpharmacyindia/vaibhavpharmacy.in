Lucknow Ki Garmi Mein Dehydration Se Kaise Bachein: Complete Guide

March 21, 2026 6 min read By Expert Pharmacist

Lucknow ki garmi ka apna alag hi level hai. April se June tak temperature 45 degree cross karna aam baat hai. Aur is garmi mein sabse zyada jo problem hoti hai woh hai dehydration.

Dehydration matlab aapke body mein paani ki kami ho jaana. Sunne mein simple lagta hai, lekin agar seriously na liya jaaye toh yeh life-threatening ho sakta hai, especially bachche aur buzurg logon ke liye.

Dehydration Ke Signs Jo Log Miss Kar Dete Hain

Zyaadatar log sochte hain ki "pyaas lagi toh paani pi lenge." Problem yeh hai ki jab tak pyaas lagti hai, body already 1-2% dehydrated ho chuki hoti hai. Yeh hain woh signs jo aapko pehle dhyan dene chahiye:

  • Dark yellow urine: Agar aapka peshab gahre peele rang ka hai, toh aap already dehydrated ho. Normal hydrated urine halke peele ya almost clear hota hai.
  • Thakan bina kisi wajah ke: Office mein baithe baithe neend aa rahi hai? Kaam mein man nahi lag raha? Yeh garmi ka asar nahi, yeh dehydration ho sakta hai.
  • Sar dard jo shaam tak badh jaaye: Dehydration se brain temporarily shrink hota hai (haan, literally), jisse headache hota hai.
  • Sukhi hothon aur dry skin: Lip balm lagane se kaam nahi chalega agar body ke andar paani hi nahi hai.
  • Chakkar aana: Aankhon ke aage andhera chha jana - yeh severe dehydration ka sign hai. Agar yeh ho raha hai toh turant medical attention chahiye.

ORS: Sasta, Simple, Lifesaver

ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts) sabse underrated medicine hai jo har ghar mein honi chahiye. Yeh sirf paani nahi deta, yeh paani ke saath sodium, potassium aur glucose deta hai jo body ko rehydrate karne ke liye zaruri hain.

Kab ORS lena chahiye:

  • Loose motions ya vomiting ke baad.
  • Zyada paseena aane ke baad (outdoor kaam, exercise).
  • Heatstroke ke initial signs mein.
  • Agar plain paani se thakan dur nahi ho rahi.

Ghar pe ORS kaise banayein: 1 litre saaf paani mein 6 chammach cheeni aur aadha chammach namak. Bas. Yeh WHO-approved formula hai.

Kya Peena Chahiye, Kya Nahi

Peena Chahiye:

  • Paani: Obvious, lekin reminder zaruri hai, din mein 3-4 litre.
  • Nariyal paani: Natural electrolytes milte hain.
  • Nimbu paani: Namak aur cheeni ke saath.
  • Chhaachh / lassi: Jisme probiotics bhi hote hain.
  • Tarbuz, kheera: High water content fruits.

Mat Piyo:

  • Cold drinks / soda: Sugar dehydrate karta hai.
  • Bahut zyada chai / coffee: Caffeine diuretic hai (urine output badhata hai).
  • Alcohol: Severely dehydrating hota hai.

Bachche Aur Buzurg: Extra Dhyan Zaruri

Bachche aksar khelte mein itna busy ho jaate hain ki paani peena bhool jaate hain. Unhe har 30-45 minute mein yaad dilana padta hai. Agar bachcha normal se zyada chidhchidha ho raha hai, ro raha hai lekin aankhon se aansu nahi aa rahe, ya diaper 6+ ghante se sukha hai, toh yeh dehydration ke signs hain.

Buzurg logon mein pyaas ka sensation kam ho jaata hai age ke saath. Isliye unhe schedule bana ke paani dena chahiye, unke pyaas lagne ka wait mat karo.
