## Competitive Moat (What makes us unbeatable)

By end of Month 3, Vaibhav Pharmacy will have:

1. **80+ Google Reviews** with 4.8★ rating (social proof)
2. **17 SEO blog posts** ranking for local pharmacy keywords (competitor has zero)
3. **Active Instagram** with health reels in Hinglish (relatability)
4. **Prescription reminder system** (stickiness — customers can't leave)
5. **Referral network** (organic growth engine)
